About Us

Repurposa was born from the frustration of a solo content creator who was constantly burnt out trying to keep up with editing and posting on multiple platforms. We know how exhausting it can be to stay visible online, especially when you’re managing everything alone.

That’s why Repurposa is built to be your silent content repurposing partner. We use automation, AI, and expert human editors to take a single long-form video and turn it into multiple high-performing, platform-optimised clips — while you focus on creating.

Our mission is to give creators their time back, make consistent posting effortless, and help grow faceless or personal brands without burnout. Whether you’re a busy entrepreneur, podcaster, educator or a creator who just wants to stay consistent — Repurposa is built for you.
